Triumph and Tragedy — Offensive Turns and Blitz Variants

These variants come from the desire to simulate some of the more dynamic and drastic operations that happened historically. They alow for more potential movement each season. The first—Offensive turns—has proven to work very well in Triumph & Tragedy's sister game, Conquest & Consequence, and is on the road to becoming an official part of the rules for that game. Blitz turns are more... experimental, and exactly how they work (What unlocks the ability to use them? What are their restrictions? How 'official' are they?) remains subject to change.

Offensive Turns:

Players can play two valid Command cards in normal Turn Order. Once revealed, the earlier (A-Z) card is operative; the later card adds an additional 4 Command Value.

  • Any off Season cards are discarded as bluffs.
  • A USSR Offensive Turn in Winter requires 2 cards of the same Season.

Blitz Turns:

Players with either the Heavy Tanks or Motorized Infantry Technology can perform Command Blitz Turns (2 Turns/Season) by playing three valid Seasonal Command Cards, played singly in normal Turn Order (three consecutive “Passes” still ends the Command Phase). The earliest and latest [A-Z] Command Cards are both operative that Season, each commanding a normal Player Turn in its normal Command Priority Order. The middle Command Card is discarded to enable the second Player Turn in that Season.

  • The Offensive Turns variant always included with Blitz Turns
  • Any off Season cards are discarded as bluffs.
  • No Strategic Movement is allowed in either portion of a Blitz Double Turn.
  • USSR Winter Blitz Turns require 3 cards for the same Season.
